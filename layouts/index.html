Overview

Welcome to the home page of the Swiss blockchain summer school 2017. The event is jointly organised by EPFL and ETHZ and aims at bringing together students (Master- and PhD-level), academic researchers, and security experts from industry with an interest in cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology.

The school is co-located with the EPFL IC summer research institute.

Application

To apply, please click on the button below and fill out the form. We reserve the right to select from the list of applicants based on the provided motivation and experience if the number of registrations is higher than the available seats.

The application closes May 12th and confirmations will be sent out shortly afterwards.
